Royal Bengal Tiger

The Royal Bengal Tiger, scientifically known as Panthera tigris tigris, is a majestic big cat native to the Indian subcontinent. It is characterized by its distinctive orange coat with black stripes, powerful build, and keen hunting abilities. Unfortunately, this endangered species faces threats such as habitat loss and poaching. Conservation efforts are vital to protect its dwindling population and preserve the natural beauty of these regal creatures.
